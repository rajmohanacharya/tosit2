Warranty Policy

1 Year Warranty on Refurbished Laptops

At The One Source IT Solutions, we provide comprehensive warranty coverage on all our refurbished laptops to ensure your complete peace of mind. Our warranty program is designed to protect your investment and provide exceptional after-sales support.

Warranty Coverage

Our 12-month warranty covers all hardware components and ensures reliable performance:

Hardware Components Covered

  • Motherboard: Complete protection against component failures
  • Processor: CPU performance and functionality guarantee
  • Memory (RAM): Full coverage for memory modules
  • Storage: Hard disk drives and SSD protection
  • Display: Screen functionality and backlight coverage
  • Keyboard & Touchpad: Input device functionality
  • Audio System: Speakers and audio output
  • Ports & Connectivity: USB, HDMI, and other ports
  • Battery: Minimum 70% capacity guarantee
  • Charging System: Power adapter and charging port

Warranty Terms & Conditions

What's Covered:

  • Manufacturing defects in hardware components
  • Component failures during normal usage
  • Pre-installed operating system issues
  • Free repair or replacement of defective parts
  • Technical support via phone and email

What's Not Covered:

  • Physical damage due to accidents, drops, or spills
  • Damage from misuse, abuse, or negligence
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Software issues not related to pre-installed OS
  • Cosmetic wear and normal aging
  • Damage from power surges or electrical issues
  • Loss of data or software

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a refurbished laptop?

A refurbished laptop is a pre-owned device that has been professionally restored to like-new condition. Each laptop undergoes comprehensive testing, cleaning, component replacement (if needed), and quality certification before being offered for sale.

What is your quality assurance process?

Every laptop goes through our rigorous 15-point inspection process including hardware diagnostics, performance testing, cosmetic evaluation, battery health check, and comprehensive software testing. Only devices meeting our strict quality standards are certified for sale.

What warranty do you provide?

All refurbished laptops come with a comprehensive 12-month warranty covering all hardware components, including motherboard, processor, RAM, storage, display, and battery. We also provide 15 days return policy for complete satisfaction.

How do I sell my laptop to you?

Our selling process is simple: (1) Provide your laptop details online, (2) Answer condition assessment questions, (3) Receive instant quote, (4) Schedule free pickup. We handle evaluation, payment processing, and provide instant payment upon verification.

How quickly do you process payments for sold laptops?

Payments are processed within 24 hours of laptop verification at our facility. We offer multiple payment options including bank transfer, UPI, and cash payment during pickup. You'll receive payment confirmation via SMS and email.

Do you provide free pickup and delivery?

Yes! We offer completely free doorstep pickup for laptop sales and free delivery for purchases within Bangalore and major Indian cities. For remote locations, nominal shipping charges may apply, which will be clearly communicated upfront.

Can I upgrade RAM or storage after purchase?

Many of our laptops support hardware upgrades. We provide upgrade services for RAM, storage (SSD/HDD), and other compatible components. Contact our technical team at +91-9876543210 for compatibility check and upgrade pricing.

What if I'm not satisfied with my purchase?

We offer a 15-day no-questions-asked return policy. If you're not completely satisfied, return the laptop in original condition with all accessories and packaging for a full refund. Return pickup is completely free.

What operating system comes with the laptops?

All laptops come with genuine Windows 10/11 or Linux (Ubuntu) pre-installed and activated. We provide original OS installation media and license documentation. macOS is available on Apple laptops only.

Do you offer EMI options?

Yes, we offer flexible EMI options through leading banks and financial partners. EMI plans start from 6 months to 24 months with competitive interest rates. Zero down payment options available for select customers.

What about data security on returned laptops?

We follow strict data sanitization protocols. All storage devices undergo multiple-pass data wiping using military-grade software to ensure complete data destruction. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

Can I exchange my current laptop for a different model?

Yes! We offer laptop exchange services. Get your current laptop evaluated, receive instant quote, and apply the value toward purchasing a different model from our inventory. Exchange value is adjusted against the new laptop price.

What accessories are included with the laptop?

Each laptop comes with original or compatible power adapter, battery, and basic documentation. Original accessories like bags, mice, or additional software depend on the specific model and will be clearly mentioned in the product description.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive tracking information via SMS and email. You can track your shipment in real-time using the provided tracking number. Our customer service team is also available for status updates.
